The challenge instance unsurprisingly displayed the contents of [RFC 9309](https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/html/rfc9309).

![](./files/07%20-%20RFC%209309/01.png)

Upon requesting `/robots.txt` I noticed there were hundreds of blank lines being returned. The flag was somewhere inside.

```
>>> import urllib.request, re
>>> page = urllib.request.urlopen("http://10.1.136.228/robots.txt").read().decode().strip()
>>> print(re.findall(r"flag\{.+?\}", page)[0])
flag{aec1142c199aa5d8ad0f3ae3fa82e13c}
```

Flag: **flag{aec1142c199aa5d8ad0f3ae3fa82e13c}**
